Who We Are

E-Built is a Philadelphia based Construction Management firm that is committed to utilizing building practices that are sustainable, energy efficient and socially responsible. We pride ourselves in being a high quality CM/GC firm that provides solutions for our clients that meet their budgetary needs.

Founded in 2008 as the construction division of a development group, E-Built quickly expanded to offer a wide array of services to residential, commercial and institutional clients in the Tri-State Area. Coming from a development background, E-Built is acutely aware of the needs of their clients and works diligently to fulfill the needs of the client and strengthen the client / builder relationship.
